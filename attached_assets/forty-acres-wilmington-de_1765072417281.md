
# Forty Acres
## WILMINGTON, DELAWARE

## 1. SEO Intro Section
Forty Acres is one of Wilmington’s most charming and historic neighborhoods—defined by its tree-lined streets, well-preserved brick rowhomes, and a strong sense of community. Located just west of downtown and adjacent to both Trolley Square and the Highlands, Forty Acres offers residents an ideal blend of residential calm and walkable urban convenience. With its proximity to Rockford Park, top schools, and vibrant commercial districts, the neighborhood is consistently one of Wilmington’s most desirable places to live.

As a Wilmington-market specialist, Kevin helps buyers navigate Forty Acres’ unique mix of historic character, updated interiors, and highly walkable surroundings. Whether you're looking for a cozy rowhome, a renovated historic property, or a home steps from Trolley Square’s amenities, Forty Acres delivers an appealing lifestyle with long-term value.

## 1. SEO Intro Section
Centreville, Delaware (often misspelled as **Centerville**) is a picturesque, upscale village located in the Brandywine Valley—known for its rolling green landscapes, historic estates, boutique shops, and timeless rural charm. Just minutes from Greenville and the Pennsylvania border, Centreville offers a luxurious and peaceful lifestyle while remaining convenient to Wilmington, Hockessin, and Kennett Square.

With scenic views, large-lot homes, and proximity to the area’s top attractions, Centreville appeals to buyers seeking high-end living in a quiet, exclusive setting.

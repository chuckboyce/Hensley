
# Highlands
## WILMINGTON, DELAWARE

## 1. SEO Intro Section
The Highlands is one of Wilmington’s most prestigious and architecturally rich neighborhoods—defined by its stately homes, mature trees, historic character, and unmatched proximity to the city’s finest parks and cultural attractions. Bordering both Rockford Park and Brandywine Park, the Highlands offers residents a serene, upscale residential environment while remaining minutes from downtown Wilmington, Trolley Square, and Greenville.

Known for its elegant early-20th-century homes, quiet streets, and strong community identity, the Highlands attracts buyers seeking luxury living with historic charm. As a Wilmington-area expert, Kevin guides clients through the neighborhood’s unique, high-value properties—many featuring custom details, stone architecture, and large, beautifully landscaped lots. For those seeking premium Wilmington living, the Highlands stands at the top of the list.

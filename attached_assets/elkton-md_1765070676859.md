
# Elkton
## MARYLAND

## 1. SEO Intro Section
Elkton, Maryland—known as the “Gateway to the Chesapeake Bay”—is a thriving town located just across the Delaware border. With its historic downtown, convenient access to Newark and Middletown, and proximity to major travel routes like I-95 and Route 40, Elkton attracts homebuyers seeking a blend of suburban comfort and rural charm. Elkton offers a wide range of home styles, spacious lots, scenic landscapes, and quick access to outdoor recreation along the Elk and Big Elk Creeks.

As a regional real estate expert serving both Delaware and Maryland, Kevin helps clients explore Elkton’s diverse neighborhoods, from established communities to newer developments with modern amenities. Whether you're searching for a starter home, a move-up property with land, or a waterfront retreat, Elkton provides excellent value and accessibility for buyers looking in the greater Newark–Middletown region.

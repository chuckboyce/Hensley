
# Perryville
## MARYLAND

## 1. SEO Intro Section
Perryville, Maryland is a scenic waterfront town located along the Susquehanna River, known for its peaceful neighborhoods, access to the MARC commuter rail, and proximity to both Aberdeen and Elkton. With a blend of small-town charm, river views, and major transportation connections, Perryville is an attractive choice for homebuyers seeking convenience, affordability, and natural beauty. Its location near I-95 makes it a smart option for commuters working in Maryland or Delaware.

As a regional real estate expert, Kevin helps buyers explore Perryville’s appealing neighborhoods, including popular waterfront areas, established subdivisions, and modern residential communities near the casino and shopping corridors. Whether you're looking for a riverfront home, a suburban property with a yard, or a convenient commuter location, Perryville offers strong value and lifestyle appeal.
